Introduction to our school

Welcome

Welcome to our school! We are committed to spreading the light of education. Our mission is to provide quality education to every student and ensure their emotional, social and moral development.

Our purpose

Our main objective is to enlighten the students with the light of knowledge. We believe that education can create a better and civilized society. We create an environment where students can think independently and encourage creativity.

Curriculum

Our school provides education from primary to secondary level. We follow the National Curriculum and also conduct various creative activities and co-curricular activities for the students. Our school has modern science labs, computer labs, libraries and playgrounds for holistic development of students.

Teacher

Every teacher in our school is highly qualified, experienced and dedicated to the students. They are not limited to teaching but also focus on personal and social development of students. They are always enthusiastic and helpful in developing students' intelligence and thinking.

Parent connection

We believe that a strong relationship between teachers and parents is crucial for student development. Hence we regularly organize parent meetings, workshops and various seminars where parents can share their views and suggestions.

Co-curricular activities

Apart from education, our school has various co-curricular activities like cultural programs, science fairs, debate competitions, sports competitions etc. These activities help students to increase their confidence and develop leadership skills.

Infrastructure

Our school infrastructure is modern and well equipped. Each classroom is spacious and well ventilated. We have advanced technology based classrooms where teaching is done through smart boards.
